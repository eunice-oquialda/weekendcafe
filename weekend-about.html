Meet the Weekend

Tucked in the heart of Rosedale, The Weekend Cafe has been on top of bringing quality and sustainable food, good vibe, local produce to its community. Located in the historic building “The Esplanade”, we honour tradition by supporting the community through the use of local farmers and artisans.

From the heart of our kitchen comes curated dishes, for the city crowd. We aim to bring fresh food, amazing brunch hour, aromatic coffee, and gifts you can bring to friends or even just at home.

Brunch is not just brunch. It is a lifestyle. A moment in time - to enjoy, and worry about nothing but what IPA is good with your waffles. We are here to serve it to you on weeekends, but even just in ordinary days.

Everyday is a weekend, at The Weekend Cafe!
